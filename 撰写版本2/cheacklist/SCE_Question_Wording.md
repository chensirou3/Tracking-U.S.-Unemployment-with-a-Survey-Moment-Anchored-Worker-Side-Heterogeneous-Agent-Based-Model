3. The role the variable plays in the agent population.
4. A placeholder line where the official wording must be inserted.
5. The sample period covered by the file actually loaded by Phase 2.

## Honesty caveat (critical)

The audit of `population_init_engine.py` (see `ABM_P1_Resolution_Report.md` §4) shows that the population is parameterised by **hand-tuned distributional families with parameters informed by these SCE moments**, not by direct resampling of SCE rows. The paper must say "survey-moment-anchored", **not** "survey-sampled" or "calibrated to SCE microdata". Some of the variables in the table above are listed in `extract_distributions.py` for diagnostic moment computation only, and never enter the executable population draw.
